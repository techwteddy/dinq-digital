Our Story

Built in Ethiopia.
Built for the World.

Dinq Digital was born from a simple belief — Ethiopian businesses deserve world-class digital products, not second-rate templates. We saw local companies struggling with expensive foreign agencies that didn't understand the market, the language, or the culture.

So we built the studio we wished existed. One that speaks Amharic and English, integrates Telebirr and Stripe, and designs for both the streets of Addis Ababa and the boardrooms of London and Dubai.

Today we build websites, SaaS products, and digital experiences for Ethiopian businesses going global — and international brands entering Africa's fastest-growing market.

🇪🇹 Ethiopia First

We understand the local market, networks, and payment systems better than anyone.

🌍 Global Standards

Every product we ship meets international design and performance benchmarks.

⚡ Speed Matters

We move fast without cutting corners. Most projects launch in under 30 days.
