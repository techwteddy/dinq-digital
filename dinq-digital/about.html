Who We Are

We build the system

Dinq Digital is an Ethiopian web design and software studio. We don't just build websites — we build the operating systems that run behind them. CRM, payments, bookings, analytics, AI — all connected.

Founded in Addis Ababa, serving clients across Ethiopia, the US diaspora, and beyond. Every project we ship is production-ready, fast, and built to grow.
