About Dinq Digital

Ethiopian Design.
Global Reach.

Dinq Digital is an Ethiopian web design and software studio. We build digital products that are beautiful, fast, and built to convert — rooted in Ethiopian culture, engineered for the world.

We are the creators of DinqOS — a 42-module business operating system spanning 15 verticals, from salons and auto repair shops to logistics, diaspora shipping, and restaurant management.

We also produce brand videos, run social media campaigns, and manage paid ads — everything a growing business needs under one roof.

DinqOS Platform

DinqOS is a 42-module multi-tenant business operating system built for Ethiopian and diaspora businesses. Verticals include DinqBook (salons), DinqShop (auto repair), DinqCargo (diaspora shipping), DinqServe (restaurants), and 11 more.

E-Commerce

Full e-commerce builds with product management, inventory, checkout, and payment integration. Built for Ethiopian retail businesses ready to sell online.

Video Production

Brand videos, social reels, product showcases, and promotional content. We handle concept, filming, and editing — delivering content that stops the scroll and tells your story.

Social Media & Ads

We run your social media presence and paid ad campaigns end-to-end. Content calendars, post creation, community management, Meta Ads, and monthly analytics reporting.

Growth / SEO
